about

My name is Johannes Ekengren, and I am a software engineer. I am coming up on 10 years as a software engineer and 6 of those years in the video game industry. I have worked on 3 AAA video game titles, Need for Speed Heat, Battlefield 2042 and Need for Speed Unbound. At my latest job I helped build a game called Godbreakers, which were composed of a smaller team where I had a big impact on shaping the game. It truly was a passion project which I am very proud of.

Please reach out if you want to work together or just want to connect, and please check out my work experience and projects down below.

Best regards, Johannes Ekengren
